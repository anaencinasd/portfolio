About Me

Hi there! I'm Ana, a fullstack web developer with a master's degree in UX/UI design. I have skills in web development, both on the server-side and the client-side. I'm proficient in both backend and frontend technologies. I have experience in programming with CSS, HTML, JavaScript, React, PHP, and Laravel, as well as handling databases with SQL. I also hold a degree in journalism and specialized in institutional and corporate communication.

My passion lies in creating engaging and meaningful digital experiences for users. I adopt a user-centered approach, which involves constantly considering their needs and desires when designing any kind of experience. Through research, analysis, and collaboration with users and the team, I can combine my design and development skills to create innovative and effective solutions.

My goal is to exceed user expectations, surprise them, and delight them. With my UX design skills and growing experience in frontend development, I can translate creative ideas into intuitive and appealing interfaces. I'm passionate about continuous learning and applying new technologies and techniques to deliver exceptional digital experiences.
